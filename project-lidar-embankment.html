with LiDAR

A complete, reproducible drone LiDAR pipeline for river embankment condition assessment , from raw point cloud to a spatially referenced database of 4,224 segments with elevation, crest width, and slope statistics at 100-metre resolution.

Overview

River embankments , locally called polders , are the primary flood defence across southwestern Bangladesh's coastal delta. This project builds a complete pipeline from raw drone LiDAR point clouds to a spatially referenced database of embankment condition metrics, with each 100-metre segment characterised by elevation, crest width, and slope statistics.

Project Context , This pipeline was developed as part of the SURF-IT project, whose end goal is an AI-driven early warning system for tidal surge. The embankment condition database feeds directly into that vulnerability assessment model.

Pipeline

I executed this end-to-end geospatial workflow, from field drone deployment to a fully analysis-ready CSV and shapefile, under the constant guidance and supervision of Dr. Md. Farhadur Rahman.

01

Field · DJI Drone + RTK GNSS

Drone LiDAR surveys and RTK ground control across multiple grid areas in the study site.

02

DJI Terra

Reconstruction of 2D orthomosaic maps and 3D LiDAR point clouds from raw scan data.

03

CloudCompare

Point cloud cleaning , removing vegetation, structures, and noise , followed by DEM export at 50cm resolution.

04

ArcMap (ArcGIS Desktop)

DEM projection, clipping, and mosaicking; perpendicular cross-section generation at 100m intervals.

05

R · DEM_height_correction_manual.R

Sequential elevation correction using SRTM reference data and RTK ground-truth points.

06

R · Segment_data_analysis_stat_summary.R

Computes per-segment statistics , crest width, slope, and elevation , and outputs the final segment database.

R Scripts

DEM_height_correction_manual.R

Corrects elevation offsets across LiDAR-derived DEM tiles using three sequential methods:

  • SRTM correction , median dZ with top-25% trimming
  • RTK correction , centimetre-accurate per-tile offset
  • Overlap correction , for tiles without RTK coverage
terra sf
Segment_data_analysis_stat_summary.R

Joins four cross-section intersection files and computes per-segment geometry and statistics:

  • Width, vertical and horizontal distances
  • Slopes in degrees and percentage
  • Quality filtering + 7-point gap fill
dplyr zoo
